Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the competition

Who is eligible to participate?

All currently enrolled LAUTECH students from any faculty or department are eligible. Both undergraduate and postgraduate students can participate. Teams must consist of 2-4 students currently enrolled at LAUTECH.

What are the key dates and deadlines?

Key dates include: Registration closes on January 30, Preliminary submissions due February 28, Semi-finals on March 10, and Campus Pitch Day on March 20. All deadlines are at 11:59 PM WAT.

What resources are available for participants?

Participants receive mentorship from industry experts, access to workshop series on social entrepreneurship, networking opportunities with past winners and judges, and resources on UN Sustainable Development Goals.

What are the judging criteria?

Submissions are judged on innovation (30%), scalability (25%), social impact (25%), feasibility (15%), and alignment with UNLIMITED theme (5%). Detailed rubric available upon registration.
